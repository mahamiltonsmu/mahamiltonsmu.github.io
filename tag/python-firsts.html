plt.show()

plt.show()

plt.spy(M)
plt.show()

print("Number of nonzero elements in L:", np.count_nonzero(L) )
print("Number of nonzero elements in U:", np.count_nonzero(U))

P is a permutation of the identity matrix; it's a record of all the row interchanges done in the LU factorization of A. Because P is the identity matrix exactly, we know no pivoting was involved in the LU factorization of A.
